  • How can I participate in the event?

    You must have a registered team in one of the divisions available. Please visit registration
    section of the website (click HERE). See the rules and regulations page five for a description
    of the different types of divisions (click HERE).

  • How can I volunteer in the event?

    Many organizations lend a helping hand to the festival. You may contact these organizations to
    volunteer for the festival. American Red Cross, Auxiliary Police, Bravo, Civil Air Patrol,
    Goldman Sachs, Stuyvesant ARISTA, New York Downtown Hospital and NY Wall Street Dragons Cultural
    Foundation are some of the groups that have volunteered in the past.
